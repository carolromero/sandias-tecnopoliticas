Tecnosandías is a multilateral impulse of technopolitical solidarity with Palestine, expressed through this website and supported by numerous local and international projects and collectives. Its objective is to contribute to the anti-colonial cause of the Palestinian struggle for human rights (always in intersection with so many other struggles), intervening against the alliance between the Zionist State of Israel and the industrial-cultural complex of Big Tech. Tecnosandías boosts diverse initiatives, starting with its Statement.

Supporting one of our actions, campaigns or writings does not imply membership in the network or support for the rest of our activities.

If you want to join us, you can do so by writing to tecnosandias@riseup.net.
